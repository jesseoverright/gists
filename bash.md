### SCP

Local to Remote

    $ scp file.txt username@remote.com:/path/to/file.txt

Remote to Local

    $ scp username@remote.com:/path/to/file.txt /path/to/local/file.txt

### SSH

Execute a command remotely

    $ ssh username@remote.com 'ls -l /home/directory/'
